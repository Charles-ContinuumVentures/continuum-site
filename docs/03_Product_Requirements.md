# Continuum Ventures Website Product Requirements

## Purpose

The Continuum Ventures website is the public-facing digital headquarters for the company.

Its primary objectives are to:

* Establish credibility and trust.
* Explain the company's mission and vision.
* Showcase current and future ventures.
* Publish valuable insights and thought leadership.
* Generate partnership and business opportunities.
* Provide a scalable foundation for future growth.

---

# Goals

## Business Goals

* Establish Continuum Ventures as a credible venture-building company.
* Introduce the company's portfolio of ventures.
* Generate inbound business inquiries.
* Build long-term brand authority.
* Support future ventures with a shared platform and design system.

---

## User Goals

Visitors should be able to:

* Understand what Continuum Ventures does within seconds.
* Learn about each venture.
* Read educational content and company updates.
* Contact the company easily.
* Explore future projects.

---

# Primary Navigation

* Home
* About
* Ventures
* Insights
* Contact

# Homepage Requirements

The homepage should include:

## Hero

Headline:

**Building businesses that solve real problems.**

Supporting copy explaining what Continuum Ventures is and how it creates practical businesses and technology-enabled solutions.

Primary CTA:

**Explore Our Ventures**

Secondary CTA:

**Learn More**

---

## Featured Ventures

Display venture cards including:

* TravelTeamFinder.com (Coming Soon)
* Local Business AI (In Development)
* Future Ventures (Planned)

Each card should include:

* Name
* Short description
* Current status
* Learn More button

---

## Mission Section

Brief explanation of:

* Why Continuum Ventures exists
* Long-term vision
* Commitment to building practical businesses

---

## Insights Preview

Display the latest articles with:

* Title
* Date
* Short excerpt
* Read More link

---

## Call to Action

Invite visitors to:

* Start a conversation
* Explore partnerships
* Contact Continuum Ventures

---

# About Page

Should include:

* Company story
* Mission
* Vision
* Values
* Approach to venture building
* Long-term philosophy

The emphasis should remain on the company rather than an individual founder.

---

# Ventures Page

Display all ventures in a consistent card layout.

Each venture should include:

* Logo (when available)
* Status
* Description
* Category
* Link to dedicated page (future)

---

# Insights

Support publishing articles covering:

* Entrepreneurship
* Technology
* AI
* Business strategy
* Venture updates
* Product development
* Lessons learned

The content should strengthen Continuum Ventures' reputation and support long-term SEO.

---

# Contact

Include:

* Contact form
* Business email
* Optional LinkedIn link
* Clear response expectations

---

# Functional Requirements

The website should provide:

* Responsive design
* Fast page loading
* Accessible navigation
* Search engine optimization
* Sitemap generation
* Metadata for every page
* Social sharing previews
* Mobile-first experience

---

# Non-Functional Requirements

The website should be:

* Fast
* Secure
* Maintainable
* Accessible
* Scalable
* SEO-friendly
* Easy to extend as new ventures launch

---

The website should ultimately be measured by:
